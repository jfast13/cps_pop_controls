CPS Population Control Adjustments

Current Population Survey — annual benchmark adjustments, 1996–2026

About the data: Each year the BLS applies a population control adjustment to the CPS to align survey estimates with updated Census Bureau population benchmarks. The values shown are the size of that annual adjustment (in thousands of persons). Data was manually collected from the BLS CPS Technical Documentation page. No adjustments were published for 2000–2002. Red dashed lines mark years when the CPS adopted a new Census-based population estimate series.
